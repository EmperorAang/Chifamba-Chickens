Our Story

Chifamba Chickens is a family-owned and operated business dedicated to providing fresh, high-quality poultry to our community. We started our journey in 2022, with a small farm and a passion for raising healthy chickens.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted supplier of poultry products, known for our commitment to sustainable farming practices and the welfare of our animals. We believe in providing our customers with not only delicious chicken, but also peace of mind knowing it's raised with care.

Our Mission & Values

Mission Statement

To provide our customers with the freshest, healthiest, and most flavorful poultry products possible, while upholding ethical and sustainable farming practices.

Core Values

  • Quality: We are committed to providing the highest quality poultry products.
  • Sustainability: We believe in responsible farming practices that protect the environment.
  • Animal Welfare: We prioritize the well-being of our chickens.
  • Community: We value our role in supporting our local community.
